# Roulette Sync

A Geode mod that connects your Geometry Dash game to the **GD Roulette PvP** website for verified competitive play.

## How to Use

1. Open the Roulette PvP website and join/create a **Verified** lobby.
2. Copy your **Connection Token** from the lobby screen.
3. In Geometry Dash, open the Geode mod menu and find **Roulette Sync**.
4. Paste your token into the settings.
5. Play! Your progress will be automatically reported to the website.

## What It Does

- Detects when you die or complete a level
- Reports your exact percentage to the PvP server
- Prevents cheating by verifying completions through actual gameplay
